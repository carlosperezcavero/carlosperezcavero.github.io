Carlos Pérez-Cavero

Hola! I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the London School of Economics and Political Science, where I am affiliated with STICERD. I work on topics in development and environmental economics, with a particular focus on the development and environmental effects of extractive industries.

As a Peruvian development economist, I am committed to generating evidence that addresses the country's economic challenges and contributes to improving living standards. Hence, some of my work features empirical studies set in Peru.
