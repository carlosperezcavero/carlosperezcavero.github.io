I am a MRes/Phd student in Economics at the [London School of Economics and Political Science](https://www.lse.ac.uk/economics) (LSE). Before joining LSE, I worked as a Pre-doctoral Research Fellow in the [Vancouver School of Economics at UBC](https://economics.ubc.ca/) where I also completed a MA in Economics. I received my BA in Economics from the [Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú](https://www.pucp.edu.pe/). My research focuses on development economics, environmental economics, and applied econometrics.
